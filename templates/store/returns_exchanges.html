Return Policy

We want you to love your Lussoux Hairs purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, you may return eligible items within 7 days of delivery.

  • Contact us before returning any item
  • Custom or used products cannot be returned
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable

Exchange Policy

Need a different size or color? We offer exchanges for eligible items within 7 days of delivery.

  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Contact us to initiate an exchange
  • Customer covers return shipping costs
  • We'll ship your replacement once we receive the original item
