Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Ordering from Lussoux Hairs is simple:

  1. Browse our collection of premium hair products
  2. Select your desired items and add them to your cart
  3. Proceed to checkout when ready
  4. Enter your shipping and payment details
  5. Review and confirm your order

You'll receive an order confirmation email with all the details.

We offer multiple convenient payment options:

  • Online Payments: Secure credit/debit card processing
  • WhatsApp Payments: Direct payment through WhatsApp for quick transactions
  • Bank Transfers: Available for customers who prefer direct bank payments

All transactions are encrypted for your security.

Tracking your Lussoux Hairs order is easy:

  • Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information
  • Click the tracking link in your email to see real-time updates
  • You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website

For any delivery concerns, our customer service team is available to assist you.

Our return policy is designed for your satisfaction:

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Contact our team to initiate a return
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned items

Some exclusions may apply to sale items or customized products.

To maintain your Lussoux hair's premium quality:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Gently detangle with a wide-tooth comb
  • Limit heat styling and always use heat protectant
  • Store properly when not in use (on a stand or in a silk bag)
  • Follow our detailed Hair Care Guide
