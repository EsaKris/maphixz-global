Hair Care Guide

Expert tips to keep your Lussoux hair looking flawless

Washing & Conditioning

  • Gently detangle hair before washing
  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Wash with lukewarm water (not hot)
  • Pat dry with a microfiber towel

Styling & Maintenance

  • Allow hair to air dry or use low heat
  • Use heat protectant before styling
  • Store wigs/extensions on a stand
  • Brush gently from ends to roots

Pro Tips for Long-Lasting Beauty

Nighttime Care

Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent tangling and frizz.
